Solution Based Services

Want to become part of the biggest Disaster Risk Reduction Network?
Get Started Guidelines

The Knowledge Vertical is an initiative envisaged to build a pool of experts in DRR and associated areas at the national level. The primary objective of Knowledge Vertical is to leverage the vast potential of academia for optimizing the efforts for Disaster Risk Reduction to build a disaster resilient nation.

The expert will be involved in different services/area as per the subject and related expertise with the approval of the competent authority of NIDM. NIDM will provide an honorarium as per the norms depending upon the level of services.
